how to play

a secret medical diagnosis is chosen every day. guess it — no letter hints!

each guess is ranked by semantic similarity to the answer. rank #1 means you nailed it.

🎯bullseye — rank #1, that's the answer
🔥on fire — top 5%
🌶️scorching — top 15%
😮warm — top 35%
😐lukewarm — top 60%
🥶cold — top 80%
❄️freezing — bottom 20%

switch categories to focus on a specialty. a new puzzle resets each day.
